Ray Tracer

I am implementing a simple Ray Tracing algorithm. Currently I have implemented it without reflections or refractions, however, I plan on adding those soon. The algorithm is running completely on the CPU but I am going to utilize OpenCL in the future to handle most of the computations on the GPU.
